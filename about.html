CAREER EXPEDITION

The journey of my career started with a passion for graphic design, which led me to explore video editing through YouTube. Despite facing limitations, I completed a graphic design course and secured my first local client after six months of practice. Gradually, I acquired more clients and upgraded my setup to delve into video editing. Freelancing for two years honed my skills until I landed my first job at Qinetic Network, where I discovered a deep interest in cinematography and photography. Although the job was short-lived, it provided me with a valuable network. I devoted countless hours to learning filmmaking, which eventually led to a job at Uber after university graduation. Though brief, my time at Uber was rewarding until unforeseen circumstances led to a freelance opportunity in communications. This marked the beginning of my full-time freelance career, allowing me to pursue directing music videos, interviews, premieres, and more.

I owe gratitude to my friend Mohammed Shayekh, who has been by my side since my graphic design journey began.
